Account Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 26, 2025

This page provides instructions on how to delete your Rozmova. account and explains what happens to your data when you request account deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete Account Through the App

  1. Open the Rozmova. application on your device
  2. Go to Settings or Account Settings
  3. Look for Account Management or Privacy Settings
  4. Select Delete Account or Close Account
  5. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your account deletion
  6. You may be asked to verify your identity or provide a reason for deletion

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

If you cannot find the account deletion option in the app or prefer to contact us directly, you can request account deletion by email:

Contact Information

  • Email: mykola.penkovskyi@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Required Information: Please include your email address associated with your Rozmova. account

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account Information: Your email address and any profile information
  • Usage Data: Analytics and diagnostic data associated with your account
  • Settings and Preferences: All personalized settings and app preferences

Audio Data

As stated in our Privacy Policy, audio recordings are automatically deleted within one second of processing and are never stored on our servers. Therefore, no audio data needs to be deleted as part of the account deletion process.

Important Notes

  • Permanent Action: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Processing Time: Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention)
  • Backup Systems: It may take additional time to remove your data from backup systems

Before You Delete Your Account

Before proceeding with account deletion, please consider:

  • You will lose access to all Rozmova. features and services
  • Any purchased content or subscriptions will be forfeited
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Rozmova. again in the future
  • Consider temporarily deactivating your account instead, if this option is available

Alternative: Data Export

If you want to keep a copy of your data before deletion, you can request a data export by contacting us at mykola.penkovskyi@gmail.com with the subject "Data Export Request".

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data (right to erasure)
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Restrict processing of your personal data
  • Data portability

Need Help?

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance with the process, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: mykola.penkovskyi@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We typically respond within 48 hours
